    Classes – Overview¶

    Tier I¶

    Acolyte

    Description

    Acolytes of the Eternal Light can heal allies through prayers to the moon goddess Luna and, with the support of the sun god Sol, deal considerable light damage in combat.

    Prerequisites

    None

    Subclasses

    Moon Priest, Moon Warden, Sun Envoy, Sun Cleric

    Alchemist

    Description

    Alchemists create items and substances from magical materials that can be used as weapons or remedies. They fight using transmuted firearms and grenades.

    Prerequisites

    None

    Subclasses

    Arsenalist, Engineer, Medic

    Archer

    Description

    As the name implies, Archers primarily fight with bows and similar ranged weapons. They are not completely defenseless in melee, however.

    Prerequisites

    None

    Subclasses

    Field Archer, Hunter, Spellbow

    Bard

    Description

    Bards use ancient soul magic to strengthen allies and weaken enemies. Their spells are based on music and emotion.

    Prerequisites

    None

    Subclasses

    Siren, Skald, Troubadour

    Druid

    Description

    Druids use nature rituals to control the battlefield with wild growth, assume the form of a beast, and heal allies.

    Prerequisites

    None

    Subclasses

    Siren, Treewarden, Therianthrope, Wildkeeper

    Fighter

    Description

    Fighters can handle a variety of weapon groups and are trained in the arts of war. Their versatility makes them valuable in any adventuring party.

    Prerequisites

    None

    Subclasses

    Breaker, Field Archer, Moon Warden, Shieldbearer, Skald, Slasher, Spellblade, Stabber, War Dancer

    Marauder

    Description

    Marauders are lone fighters who create chaos on the battlefield with brutal melee attacks. Their weapons are often less “civilized” than a fighter’s, but no less deadly.

    Prerequisites

    None

    Subclasses

    Berserker, Monk

    Nox

    Description

    Followers of the shadow goddess Nox are often found in dark alleys and shabby taverns offering questionable services. They usually specialize in stealth, blades, and poisons.

    Prerequisites

    None

    Subclasses

    Assassin, Charlatan

    Shaman

    Description

    Shamans have a strong bond to the spirit world and use it to empower allies and summon elementals.

    Prerequisites

    None

    Subclasses

    Binder, Monk, Summoner

    Wizard

    Description

    Wizards wield powerful spells to inflict devastating damage to single targets and groups. Their 'intellectual tendencies' make them reliant on less fragile allies in combat.

    Prerequisites

    None

    Subclasses

    Hermetician, Kinetician, Meteorician, Thermician, Spellblade, Spellbow

    Advanced Classes¶

    Sorcerer

    Description

    WARNING: ADVANCED CLASS
    Sorcerers are self-taught spellcasters who rely entirely on their intuition to work magic. Predictably, this often results in chaos.

    Prerequisites

    None

    Subclasses

    Sorcerer II

    Tempering: First Stage

    Alias: Tempered I, Tempering I

    Description

    WARNING: ADVANCED CLASS
    Tempered strengthen their bodies by forcing raw mana through their own flesh and soul rather than shaping it externally.
    Due to the severe physical and mental strain involved in this process, Tempered are a rare sight outside of their sects' training halls.

    Prerequisites

    None

    Subclasses

    Tempering: Second Stage

    Tier II¶

    Arsenalist

    Description

    Arsenalists specialize in Transmuted Weapons, turning curiosities into deadly armaments.

    Prerequisites

    Alchemist

    Subclasses

    Alchimetician, Gunwright

    Assassin

    Description

    A shadowy assassin. Their considerable mobility makes them a feared opponent.

    Prerequisites

    Nox

    Subclasses

    Nightstalker

    Berserker

    Description

    Berserkers are marauders who give themselves over to a bloodrage and unleash frenzied attacks that wreck enemy ranks.

    Prerequisites

    Marauder

    Subclasses

    Binder

    Description

    Binders are shamans with an exceptionally strong bond to the spirit world. They can empower allies and themselves with elemental energy.

    Prerequisites

    Shaman

    Subclasses

    Breaker

    Description

    Breakers specialize in blunt weapons to break an enemy’s defenses with great force - often literally.

    Prerequisites

    Fighter,
    Blunt: Beginner

    Subclasses

    Champion, Specialist, Templar

    Charlatan

    Description

    The Charlatan is a troublesome blend of Bard and Nox. In combat, their whispers seem to come from every direction at once. Out of combat, they seem to know everything about everyone.

    Prerequisites

    Bard,
    Nox

    Subclasses

    Puppet Master

    Engineer

    Description

    Engineers are Alchemists with a fascination for Golem-building. Their creations are as versatile as they are dangerous.

    Prerequisites

    Alchemist

    Subclasses

    Field Archer

    Description

    Field Archers are archers trained for battlefield combat who sacrifice mobility for higher impact.

    Prerequisites

    Archer,
    Fighter,
    Slashing or Stabbing: Beginner

    Subclasses

    Champion, Deadshot, Marksman, Specialist, Templar

    Hermetician

    Description

    Hermeticians are Wizards who specialize in arcane and light spells.

    Prerequisites

    Wizard

    Subclasses

    Alchimetician, Magister, Magus

    Hunter

    Description

    Hunters spend much time in the wild. They are not only excellent archers but also survivalists.

    Prerequisites

    Archer

    Subclasses

    Deadshot, Marksman, Ranger

    Kinetician

    Description

    Kineticians are Wizards who specialize in stone and water spells.

    Prerequisites

    Wizard

    Subclasses

    Magister, Magus

    Medic

    Description

    Medics are Alchemists specialized in Pharmacy, capable of delivering both cures and poison.

    Prerequisites

    Alchemist

    Subclasses

    Meteorician

    Description

    Meteoricians are Wizards who specialize in air and lightning spells.

    Prerequisites

    Wizard

    Subclasses

    Magister, Magus

    Monk

    Description

    Some Marauders find inner balance and resist the bloodrage. If such a marauder turns to shamanism, they follow the path of the Monk - warriors seeking spiritual enlightenment and bodily perfection. They prefer blunt weapons or unarmed combat to avoid the sight of flowing blood.

    Prerequisites

    Marauder,
    Shaman

    Subclasses

    Wayfarer

    Moon Priest

    Description

    Moon Priests devote themselves to the moon goddess Luna to bring her grace to the world. They are not very threatening in melee but compensate with powerful buffing and healing spells.

    Prerequisites

    Acolyte

    Subclasses

    Augur

    Moon Warden

    Description

    The temple guards of Luna. Elegant fighters who consider protecting the sick and weak their holy duty.

    Prerequisites

    Acolyte,
    Fighter

    Subclasses

    Moon Dancer

    Shieldbearer

    Description

    Shieldbearers are stalwart defenders who protect less-armored party members and draw enemy attention.

    Prerequisites

    Fighter

    Subclasses

    Champion, Specialist, Templar

    Siren

    Description

    Sirens influence both creatures and plants with ancient magic and control the battlefield with their beguiling voices.

    Prerequisites

    Bard,
    Druid

    Subclasses

    Banshee

    Skald

    Description

    Skalds are warrior-bards who inspire allies in the thick of battle.

    Prerequisites

    Bard,
    Fighter

    Subclasses

    Champion, Specialist, Templar

    Slasher

    Alias: Axefighter, Swordfighter

    Description

    Slashers are experts in slashing weapons of all kinds.

    Prerequisites

    Fighter,
    Slashing: Beginner

    Subclasses

    Champion, Specialist, Templar

    Spellblade

    Description

    Spellblades are melee-focused spellcasters with the ability to transform their weapons' damage type.

    Prerequisites

    Fighter,
    Wizard,
    Slashing or Stabbing: Beginner

    Subclasses

    Champion, Specialist, Templar

    Spellbow

    Description

    A traditional elven fighting style that combines arcane talents with natural skill.

    Prerequisites

    Archer,
    Wizard

    Subclasses

    Deadshot, Marksman

    Stabber

    Alias: Spearfighter

    Description

    Stabbers are experts with stabbing weapons of all kinds.

    Prerequisites

    Fighter,
    Stabbing: Beginner

    Subclasses

    Champion, Specialist, Templar

    Summoner

    Description

    Summoners are shamans who specialize in manifesting and commanding elementals.

    Prerequisites

    Shaman

    Subclasses

    Elementalist

    Sun Cleric

    Description

    Sun Clerics follow the example of the martial sun god Sol and dedicate themselves to purging evil in holy light.

    Prerequisites

    Acolyte

    Subclasses

    Templar

    Sun Envoy

    Description

    Sun Envoys channel the burning light of Sol into mighty spells, scorching their foes with blinding radiance.

    Prerequisites

    Acolyte

    Subclasses

    Legate

    Therianthrope

    Description

    Therianthropes embrace primal shapeshifting magics, immersing themselves in the beast souls they hunt.

    Prerequisites

    Druid

    Subclasses

    Thermician

    Description

    Thermicians are Wizards who specialize in fire and ice spells.

    Prerequisites

    Wizard

    Subclasses

    Magister, Magus

    Treewarden

    Description

    Treewardens are druids focused on the plant world. They excel at hindering foes and controlling terrain.

    Prerequisites

    Druid

    Subclasses

    Ranger

    Troubadour

    Description

    Troubadours are generally seen as eccentric but harmless entertainers. The guilds know better - their bardic soul magic is a potent tool in battle, not merely spectacle.

    Prerequisites

    Bard

    Subclasses

    Virtuoso

    War Dancer

    Description

    War Dancers specialize in polearms and dominate the battlefield with elegant precision and considerable reach.

    Prerequisites

    Fighter,
    Slashing or Stabbing: Beginner

    Subclasses

    Champion, Moon Dancer, Specialist, Templar

    Wildkeeper

    Description

    Wildkeepers are druids devoted to the animal kingdom. They fight alongside summoned beasts.

    Prerequisites

    Druid

    Subclasses

    Ranger

    Tier III¶

    Alchimetician

    Description

    Alchimeticians explore the offensive application of Aether – the pseudo-element known to Wizards as the Arcane. They combine practical alchemical application with extensive magical theory.

    Prerequisites

    Arsenalist,
    Hermetician

    Subclasses

    Augur

    Description

    Cloaked in prophecy and gentle moonlight, the Augur keeps the party alive even under the most dire of circumstances.

    Prerequisites

    Moon Priest

    Subclasses

    Banshee

    Description

    Banshees torment their foes with debilitating status conditions and lure them into dangerous positioning.

    Prerequisites

    Siren

    Subclasses

    Champion

    Description

    Champions are dangerous combatants, capable of holding their own in any melee situation – but their true value lies within their ability to protect their allies by drawing and holding hostile attention.

    Prerequisites

    Any Fighter subclass,
    Blunt or Slashing or Stabbing: Intermediate,
    Defensive: Intermediate

    Subclasses

    Deadshot

    Description

    The Deadshot specializes in eliminating priority targets through focused damage, each shot more deadly than the last.

    Prerequisites

    Field Archer / Hunter / Spellbow

    Subclasses

    Elementalist

    Description

    Elementalists do not simply summon Elementals. They channel them, empowering themselves with primal mana in the process.

    Prerequisites

    Summoner

    Subclasses

    Gunwright

    Description

    Gunwrights are an immensely powerful asset for any adventuring party. Their versatility in elemental damage types lets them easily exploit vulnerabilities while avoiding resistances.

    Prerequisites

    Arsenalist

    Subclasses

    Legate

    Description

    Invested with Sol's righteous mandate, the Legate speaks for the sun – and the sun does not look kindly upon evil and corruption.

    Prerequisites

    Sun Envoy

    Subclasses

    Magister

    Description

    Originally a title associated with faculty heads at Wizard academies, Magisters tend to excel in utility magic and battlefield control.

    Prerequisites

    Hermetician or Kinetician or Meteorician or Thermician

    Subclasses

    Magus

    Description

    In the early days of the Great Empire, the title of Magus was reserved for high-ranking military spellcasters. Today, it describes Wizards with terrifying offensive capabilities and a nigh-limitless mana pool.

    Prerequisites

    Hermetician or Kinetician or Meteorician or Thermician

    Subclasses

    Marksman

    Alias: Markswoman

    Description

    The Marksman specializes in non-magical team support, filling a critical niche against well-organized opponents.

    Prerequisites

    Field Archer / Hunter / Spellbow

    Subclasses

    Moon Dancer

    Description

    As an embodiment of Luna’s protective hand, Moon Dancers are reliable defenders who support allies with shield spells and quick reactions.

    Prerequisites

    Moon Warden,
    War Dancer

    Subclasses

    Ranger

    Description

    Rangers live for the wilds, the hunt, and freedom. They are welcome in all guilds: their arrows are deadly accurate and their powerful nature rituals rival those of druid masters.

    Prerequisites

    Hunter,
    Treewarden or Wildkeeper

    Subclasses

    Specialist

    Description

    On the way to the peak of martial prowess, few Fighters can avoid becoming Specialists – frighteningly competent martial artists focused on a particular category of melee weapon.

    Prerequisites

    Any Fighter subclass,
    Blunt or Slashing or Stabbing: Intermediate

    Subclasses

    Templar

    Description

    Templars form the elite fighting force of any Sol temple. Their mere presence burns enemies with holy light.

    Prerequisites

    Any Fighter subclass,
    Sun Cleric

    Subclasses

    Virtuoso

    Description

    Virtuoso

    Prerequisites

    Troubadour

    Subclasses

    Wayfarer

    Description

    Wayfarers travel obscure corners of the world, seeking understanding of the spiritual world and their own place within it. Their considerable combat prowess is merely a side effect of a deep connection with the elements and superhuman self-control.

    Prerequisites

    Monk

    Subclasses
