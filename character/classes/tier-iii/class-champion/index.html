    Champion

    Description

    Champions are dangerous combatants, capable of holding their own in any melee situation – but their true value lies within their ability to protect their allies by drawing and holding hostile attention.

    Prerequisites

    Any Fighter subclass, Blunt or Slashing or Stabbing: Intermediate, Defensive: Intermediate
